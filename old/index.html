About Me

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student at the Graph Machine Learning Group, within the Swiss AI lab IDSIA and USI - Università della Svizzera italiana (Switzerland), under the supervision of Prof. Cesare Alippi.

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Politecnico di Milano, and I completed a double master's degree: one in High Performance Computing Engineering at Politecnico di Milano and the other in Computational Science at USI. Additionally, I participated in the EUMASTER4HPC program.

What I'm Doing

Below are some of the key areas of my research and work, which combine technical knowledge and real-world applications.

Time series Forecasting

The area of my research focused on improving the prediction of time series over time.

Irregular Data

I aim to make predictions with the presence of spatial or temporal irregularities.

Graph Neural Networks

I employ the use of spatio-temporal neural networks.

Ethical values

I conduct my research to achieve generalizable findings that lead to clear and meaningful progress in the field.
